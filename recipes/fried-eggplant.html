Easy Fried Eggplant

Description

These crispy fried eggplant slices are easy to make, quick, and so tasty. A memorable dish from my childhood and still a favorite! I serve them with a tomato dip or sliced fresh tomatoes, fresh green beans, and bread and butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 large eggplant, peeled and sliced
  • 3 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups dry bread crumbs

Steps

  1. First amigo

    Gather all ingredients. Warm oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

  2. Second amigo

    Dip eggplant slices in egg, then coat with bread crumbs.

  3. Third amigo

    Place breaded eggplant in the hot oil and fry until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

  4. Fourth amigo

    Drain on paper towels. Serve and enjoy!
